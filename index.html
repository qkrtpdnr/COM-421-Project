Welcome to the Research Paper Toolbox

The Research Paper Toolbox is designed to guide IIT graduate students through the process of writing a research paper, from choosing a topic to formatting research data.

Each module focuses on a different step of the research process and includes tools, strategies, and examples that make academic research more efficient and approachable.
